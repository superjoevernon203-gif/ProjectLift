02 / ABOUT US

Who We Are

Project Lift is a student-led initiative focused on helping our less fortunate peers through donation drives, support programs, and sustainable giving. We bring together communities to share clothing, supplies, and everyday essentials with those who need them most — while promoting kindness, unity, and impact.
